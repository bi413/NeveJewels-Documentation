
# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-07-26

## 1. What Data We Access
The App accesses the following categories of data from a connected Accounting Systems’ company, strictly on a read-only basis:

| Data Category | Examples | Access Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Financial reports | Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet | Read-only |
| Sales transactions | Invoices, sales receipts | Read-only |
| Customer records | Customer names, linked transaction history | Read-only |
| Items/products | Item names, pricing as recorded in Accounting Systems | Read-only |

## 2. How the Data Is Used
Data retrieved from Accounting Systems is used exclusively to populate internal reporting dashboards for financial and operational analysis by Neve Jewels Group staff. It is not used for marketing, advertising, resale, or any purpose outside internal business reporting.

## 3. Who Can Access This Data
Access to synced Accounting Systems data and the resulting application is restricted to individuals who authenticate via Neve Jewels Group's SSO provider. No external customer, vendor, or third party has access to this data. Internal access is limited to personnel whose roles require financial or operational reporting visibility.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
* All data in transit between the App and Intuit's [developer portal of Quickbooks Account System] servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
* OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored encrypted at rest and are never exposed to end users or logged in plaintext.
* Synced accounting data used for reporting is stored on infrastructure controlled by Neve Jewels Group, accessible only to authorized internal systems and personnel.

## 5. Data Retention
Synced Accounting Systems data is retained upto 48 months to support historical reporting and trend analysis. Upon disconnection of the App from a Accounting Systems’ company, or upon written request, retained data will be deleted in accordance with Neve Jewels Group's internal data retention schedule.

## 6. No Third-Party Sharing
Data retrieved from Accounting Systems through this App is not sold, rented, or shared with any third party. It is used solely for internal reporting purposes within Neve Jewels Group.

## 7. Data Subject Rights
Individuals whose data may be reflected within Accounting Systems -derived reports (e.g., customer records) may exercise applicable data protection rights, including access and deletion requests, by contacting: itsupport@navgrahaa.com . Requests will be handled in accordance with applicable UK data protection law (UK GDPR).

## 8. Contact
For questions regarding this addendum, the App, or how data is handled, contact: itsupport@navgrahaa.com
