# Terms of Use

**Effective Date:** 2026-07-26

## 1. Scope of the Application
This application ("the App") is an internal business intelligence and reporting tool developed and operated by Neve Jewels Group. The App connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's official API to retrieve accounting data on a read-only basis. The App does not create, modify, update, or delete any data within the connected QuickBooks Online company file.

## 2. Authorized Users
Access to the App is restricted exclusively to individuals authenticated through Single Sign-On (SSO) provided and managed by Neve Jewels Group. The App is not available to the general public, external customers, or any third party outside of the Neve Jewels Group entity. There is no self-service signup; access is provisioned solely by internal administrators.

## 3. Purpose of Use
Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used solely to generate internal financial and operational reporting dashboards. This includes, but is not limited to, profit and loss summaries, balance sheet data, invoice records, and customer/item data, for the sole purpose of supporting internal business analysis by authorized Neve Jewels Group staff.

## 4. Prohibited Use
* The App may not be used by any individual who is not an authenticated Neve Jewels Group SSO user.
* Data retrieved through the App may not be redistributed, sold, or shared with any external party.
* The App may not be used to write, alter, or delete data in any connected QuickBooks Online company file.

## 5. Disconnection
Any authorized administrator may disconnect the App from a QuickBooks Online company at any time via the in-app disconnect option. Upon disconnection, the App will cease retrieving new data, and previously synced data will be handled in accordance with the retention terms described in the Privacy Policy section below.

Neve Jewels Group may update this agreement from time to time. Continued use of the App following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
